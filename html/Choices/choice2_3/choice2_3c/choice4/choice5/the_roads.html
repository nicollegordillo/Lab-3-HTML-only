Which way Barbara!

As you prepare to embark on the journey to the military shelter, a sense of determination guides your actions. Before starting the car, you reach into the glove box, retrieving a worn map. The creases on its surface tell the tales of previous journeys, but now, it holds the potential to guide your family to safety. With a pen in hand, you meticulously mark what appears to be the fastest route to the shelter. Each intersection, highway, and landmark becomes a vital waypoint on the map, a lifeline in the chaos that surrounds you. The plan is set, and with a deep breath, you set the car in motion, the engine roaring to life in response to your decisive action. As you approach the designated route, the gravity of the situation becomes evident. The streets, once bustling with life, now stretch before you as eerily quiet corridors. The echoes of distant moans and the occasional shuffling figure cast an unsettling atmosphere over the journey. However, your focus remains on the path marked on the map, a route that promises safety and a reunion with your husband.
