At least you're together Barbara!

The engine roars, and the car surges forward into the approaching sea of zombies. However, the sheer number of undead proves overwhelming. The vehicle collides with the first wave of bodies, the impact shaking the car and creating a gruesome spectacle as limbs and torsos are thrown aside. Despite your efforts to power through, the relentless tide of zombies proves insurmountable. The car comes to a jolting stop, wedged between the relentless undead and the tunnel exit. The windows shatter under the relentless assault, and grasping hands reach inside, clawing at the interior. Panic sets in as the once-protective shell of the car is breached. In a desperate bid to shield your children, you gather them close, the echoes of their terrified breaths mingling with the cacophony of the undead. The world outside the shattered windows transforms into a nightmarish tableau, with grasping hands and hungry mouths reaching for you and your family.
